ITICEWA is the acronym for ITI Christian Employees’ Welfare Association. As indicated in the name the association is involved in the welfare activities of the Christian employees of the Bangalore unit of M/s.ITI Limited. ITI Ltd is India’s first Public Sector Unit (PSU) established in the year 1948, offering a complete range of telecom products and total solutions covering the whole spectrum of Switching, Transmission, Access and Subscriber Premises equipment.

 

In the year 1952 a handful of people came together with a vision to bring together all the Christian employees of the organization on one platform. The main objective was to foster in the minds of every member the spirit of Christian Life, service, discipline and co-operation. And also to organize and promote social, cultural activities among its members and their families, among several other things.

 

The association grew from a handful of members to large registered organization under the Karnataka Society’s Act. Our membership stands reduced to a few hundreds as recruitment has been frozen in the organisation. However, we are still an active association bustling with several programmes in a year.
